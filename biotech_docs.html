Biotechnology course learning materials

Below are materials and notes I made. All of them are made with either LaTeX or R Markdown unless stated otherwise. If the source for a piece of text is not explicitly referenced it means it comes from a lecture in most cases. Those notes are not guaranteed to be flawless and they not only may but will contain some errors of various magnitude. I of course try to make them as reliable as I can, but there will always be mistakes.

You are welcome to report mistakes by sending me an e-mail.

At the moment most of the content is in polish language but I will try to gradually add english translations. Source code for most of files can be found on my GitHub.
